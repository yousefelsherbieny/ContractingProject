About-Us

The Factory for Architectural Contracting (MSG) traces its origins back to 1975 (corresponding to 1395 H) when it began with basic tools and machinery. Over time, MSG has upgraded its equipment to align with modern industry standards. Notably, in 1982, MSG proudly introduced the first automatic block machine in Turaif, establishing itself as a leading block manufacturer in the northern border area. In 1990, MSG ventured into concrete technology by installing the region's first 24-meter concrete pump in Turiaf. Since then, MSG has successfully executed numerous large-scale projects for both local and international clients.
